Payment Process

Thank you for ordering dental treatment at our dental practice.

Payment method

After you successfully place an order, we will send payment information via the email you provided when ordering.

Please follow these steps to complete payment:

  1. Check your email to receive payment instructions.
  2. Use the payment method provided in the email.
  3. Complete the payment according to the instructions given.

Once your payment has been verified, your dental appointment will be officially confirmed.

After Activity

After dental treatment is complete, you will receive a detailed final bill if there are any additional costs that need to be paid, such as medicines or additional treatments.

You can make payments for this additional bill according to the instructions given by our team at the dental clinic.

If you have any questions or need further assistance regarding the payment process, please do not hesitate to contact us at the number listed on the Contact page.
