Our Goals

In the RevoU Batch 5 Software Engineering Capstone Project, we created a website on the topic of health. We created a website for a dentist clinic which we named Ventinove Dental Care. This website aims to schedule an appointment with a dentist via the Book an Appointment form. Users can make a schedule according to the selected time and can choose the desired doctor based on the dental problems experienced and the services required.

Our Project

Ventinove dental center is a dental care service center with guaranteed quality number 1. With our professional doctors who have expert certification, we offer a variety of quality services such as tooth filling, tooth whitening, denture fitting, tooth extraction, dental checkups, dental health consultation, professional scaling and braces installation. In order to ensure efficiency, speed and accuracy of our services. We present the Ventinove Dental Center website. Assisted by our trusted developer, we bring the Ventinove Dental Center service so that you can access it anywhere and anytime, you can also consult with our doctor whenever you need it and book an appointment anytime and anywhere with Ventinove Dental Center.
